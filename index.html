About CinemaStream

CinemaStream was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring high-quality movie streaming to audiences worldwide, completely free of charge, via YouTube Live.

We partner with major studios and independent filmmakers to legally stream movies with proper licensing. Our team works around the clock to curate the best content and ensure smooth, uninterrupted streams.

To keep our service free, we run carefully selected advertisements that help support content licensing and platform maintenance. We're committed to providing a safe, enjoyable viewing experience for all movie lovers.
